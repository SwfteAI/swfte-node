# Swfte Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Swfte is committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for all contributors, users, and community members. We value respectful collaboration and constructive feedback.

## Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Be professional and courteous in all interactions
- Provide constructive feedback and be open to receiving it
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communication channels associated with this project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the Swfte team at conduct@swfte.com. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
